⏱ 0:45 – 0:55
The 3 A's — Deep Dive

The 3 A's are more than a closing ritual. They are a framework for accountability, gratitude, and growth — and a restorative justice practice. Click each A to go deeper.

Aha 💡
"This was illuminating for me!"

An Aha is more than a surprise — it's a moment where your thinking SHIFTS. You held a belief, and something in this session cracked it open. Name your Ahas specifically because vague insight doesn't transfer to your scholars.

DEEPER PROMPTS
• What did I believe coming in that I'm reconsidering now?
• What connection did I make that I didn't expect?
• What does this Aha mean for how I'll show up in my classroom this week?
⚔️ ARMOR: HELMET — Hunt for Research · Engage in Continuous Growth
🧠 SEL: Self-Awareness
Warrior Challenge: Name your Aha AND the scholar who will feel it first.
Appreciation 🙏
"Showing gratitude to self and/or colleagues"

Appreciation is a PRACTICE of power, not politeness. In communities that have been undervalued, naming what people bring is an act of resistance. When you appreciate a colleague, you are also modeling for your scholars what it looks like to see each other fully.

DEEPER PROMPTS
• Who in this room held space for you today — and have you told them?
• What did YOU bring today that deserves to be named?
• How does authentic appreciation build your circles of influence?
⚔️ ARMOR: BREASTPLATE — Be Authentic · Act with Care · Listen and Learn
🧠 SEL: Relationship Skills · Social Awareness
Warrior Challenge: 'Good job' is not appreciation. Name what you saw, heard, or felt — specifically.
Apologies 🛡️
"Taking accountability when we don't show up as our best selves"

A warrior apology is not self-flagellation — it's accountability WITH a path forward. 'I'm sorry I was distracted' vs. 'I was distracted and my scholars deserved better. Here's what I'm changing tomorrow.' One closes the loop. One opens a new commitment.

DEEPER PROMPTS
• Is there something from this week that you owe yourself an apology for?
• Is there a scholar, colleague, or family who deserves to hear from you?
• What's the MOVE that follows the apology?
⚔️ ARMOR: SWORD — Own Your Role as a Change Agent · Respond with Boldness
🧠 SEL: Responsible Decision-Making · Self-Management
Warrior Challenge: An apology without a commitment is just a statement. What are you committing to?
🔄 RJ CONNECTION

In Restorative Justice, community repair requires naming harm, expressing accountability, and committing to change. Aha = naming the truth. Appreciation = honoring what is being rebuilt. Apologies = accountability WITH a forward commitment. This is warrior work.

⏱ 1:10 – 1:30
Write Your Own Scenario + Partner Swap
⚔️ SWORD — Own Your Role · Speak Truth to Power 🧠 Self-Awareness · Decision-Making 🌐 Creativity · Communication
DIRECTIONS

Work individually (10 min) using the 5-step scaffold below. Your scenario can be real, anticipated, or imagined — no data required. Then swap with a partner (10 min) and apply W.A.I.T + Circles to each other's scenario. The author listens silently first.

🔄 PARTNER SWAP PROTOCOL
1
Share your scenario — do NOT explain or contextualize it. Let your partner read.
2
Partner applies W.A.I.T: What are the facts? What assumptions might be present? What questions would you ask?
3
Partner maps the Circles: Control / Influence / Concern / Ecosystem.
4
Author listens silently — then responds: "What surprised you about your partner's read?"
5
Swap roles and repeat.
